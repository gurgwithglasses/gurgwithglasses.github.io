A Little Bit About Myself:

Originally from Toronto, I moved to San Antonio, Texas from Houston to pursue higher education. Currently, I am attending The Art Institute of San Antonio and working towards my Bachelor's of Science in Fashion and Retail Management. I began working at The Sporting District, San Antonio's premiere men's store, January 2015, which allowed me to experience the ups and downs of a retail business in its first year. I am also working as an intern for Wrong Side Pocket Squares, an independent pocket square company out of San Antonio. Future plans include applying to graduate programs in fashion or sustainability in the fashion industry.
